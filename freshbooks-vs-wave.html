Which Is Better?

Choose FreshBooks if:

  • You invoice clients consistently
  • You want automated reminders
  • You prefer a professional, scalable solution

Choose Wave if:

  • You are just starting out
  • You want a free tool
  • You send invoices occasionally

Final Verdict

For most freelancers, FreshBooks is the better long-term solution. Wave is a solid free alternative.
