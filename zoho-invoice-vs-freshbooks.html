Which Should You Choose?

Choose Zoho Invoice if:

  • You want a free plan with advanced features
  • You need automated workflows
  • You want multi-currency support

Choose FreshBooks if:

  • You want a professional, easy-to-use tool
  • You invoice frequently
  • You want strong customer support

Final Recommendation

For freelancers wanting automation without paying, Zoho Invoice is great. For those prioritizing simplicity and support, FreshBooks is ideal.
